Egg recipe


A hearty and protein-rich curry made with boiled eggs simmered in a spiced onion-tomato gravy — simple, flavorful, and goes great with rice or roti.

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 25 minutes
  • Total Time: ~35 minutes
  • Servings: 3–4
  • Cuisine: Indian
  • Difficulty: Easy
    • 📋 Ingredients: 🥚 For the Eggs:
  • 4–6 boiled eggs, peeled
  • Salt and turmeric for shallow frying (optional)
    • 🌶️ For the Curry:
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped or pureed
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1.5 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1.5 cups water (adjust consistency)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
    • 🔥 Steps to Cook:
      1.Boil and (Optionally) Fry Eggs Boil eggs, peel them, and optionally slit each egg slightly. Heat 1 tsp oil, sprinkle a little turmeric and salt, and lightly fry the eggs until golden. Set aside.
      2 Prepare the Masala Base Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and bay leaf. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook till raw smell goes. Add tomatoes, salt, turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder. Cook until tomatoes break down and oil separates from the masala.
      3 Make the Curry Add about 1.5 cups of water to make gravy. Bring to a boil, then gently add the boiled eggs. Simmer for 8–10 minutes, allowing eggs to absorb flavors.
    🌿 Finish & Serve Sprinkle garam masala and chopped coriander leaves. Serve hot with rice, roti, or paratha.
