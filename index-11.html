How I Cook – My Recipe Process

Cooking is a journey filled with flavor, creativity, and joy. Here’s how I bring each recipe to life in my kitchen:

🔪 1. Gather Fresh Ingredients Every great dish begins with quality. I choose fresh vegetables, spices, and other ingredients to ensure the best taste and nutrition.

📖 2. Read and Plan the Recipe I always read the full recipe first to understand the steps. Planning ahead helps save time and avoid mistakes.

🧂 3. Prep Work Chopping veggies, grinding spices, marinating ingredients — preparation is key! I do all the prep before turning on the stove.

🔥 4. Cook with Patience I cook on the right flame, stir with care, and never rush the process. Letting flavors develop slowly makes all the difference.

🧪 5. Taste and Adjust As I cook, I keep tasting and adjusting salt, spice, or tang to make sure everything is balanced and flavorful.

🍽️ 6. Presentation A dish should look as good as it tastes! I serve my recipes with care — garnished with herbs, arranged neatly, and served warm.

💬 7. Share the Joy Cooking is better when shared. I love sharing my recipes with friends, family, and now — with you!

"Cooking isn’t just about feeding the stomach — it’s about feeding the soul."
