🍚 Lemon Rice (South Indian Style)
  • Prep Time: 10 minutes.
  • Cook Time: 10 minutes.
  • Total Time: 20 minutes
  • Serving Size: 2–3 people
  • Cuisine: South Indian
  • Difficulty level: Very Easy

    • 📋 Ingredients: 🍋 Main:
  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cooled)
  • 1–2 tablespoons lemon juice (adjust to taste)
  • Salt to taste

    • 🌶️ For Tempering:
  • 1 tablespoon oil (or ghee for richer taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds (optional)
  • 1–2 dried red chilies
  • 1 green chili (slit)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon chana dal (split Bengal gram)
  • 1 tablespoon urad dal (split black gram)
  • 8–10 curry leaves
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
    • 🥜 Optional Add-ins:
  • 2 tablespoons roasted peanuts or cashews
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
    • 🔥 Steps to Cook:
      🔪 1. Prep the Ingredients Cook rice ahead of time and let it cool completely (leftover rice works great). Squeeze lemon juice and set aside. Chop green chili and gather all tempering ingredients.
      🍳 2. Temper the Spices Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add cumin seeds, chana dal, and urad dal. Sauté until golden brown. Add dried red chilies, green chili, curry leaves, and a pinch of hing. Add turmeric powder and stir quickly (don’t burn the spices).
      🍚 3. Mix with Rice Add the cooked rice to the pan and gently mix it with the tempering. Add salt and stir to combine evenly. Turn off the heat and add lemon juice. Mix well. Taste and adjust lemon or salt if needed.
    🌿 Garnish & Serve Garnish with fresh coriander and roasted peanuts or cashews. Serve with papad, pickle, or raita.
    💡 Tips:
  • Don’t add lemon juice while the pan is hot—it can turn bitter.
  • Adjust spice levels with more or fewer green chilies.
  • You can add a pinch of sugar for a sweet-sour twist.
